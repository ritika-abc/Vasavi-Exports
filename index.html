About us

Vasavi Exports – Indian Handicrafts Exporter & Artisan Product Supplier

Vasavi Exports is a Bengaluru-based Indian handicrafts exporter specializing in wooden handicrafts, cotton bags, hand block printed fabrics, and Jaipur blue pottery.

  • Exporter of authentic Indian handicrafts made by skilled artisan communities.
  • Wide product range including wooden handicrafts, textiles, and blue pottery.
  • Scalable production capacity to fulfill bulk and large-volume export orders.
